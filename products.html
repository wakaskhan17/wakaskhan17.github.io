NEXUS

AI-Powered Delivery Intelligence Engine

What is NEXUS?

NEXUS is an intelligent delivery intelligence platform that transforms how enterprises manage project governance and execution. Built on Agile-first principles, it enables real-time governance, requirement decomposition, and delivery intelligence across complex enterprise programmes.

The Problem It Solves

70% of large infrastructure projects fail due to poor early feedback and requirement misalignment. Traditional Waterfall planning becomes obsolete within weeks as priorities shift. NEXUS bridges the gap between executive strategy and team execution with AI-native governance.

Core Capabilities

✓ AI Requirement Decomposition

Converts strategic objectives into executable backlogs in seconds

✓ Real-Time Governance

Enforces governance constraints while maintaining delivery agility

✓ Risk Intelligence

Automatic dependency flagging and schedule impact analysis

✓ Agile-First Architecture

Strategic sprints with executive feedback every 2 weeks

✓ Delivery Analytics

Real-time dashboards showing programme health and trajectory

✓ Stakeholder Alignment

Continuous executive visibility into strategic delivery progress

Market Opportunity

TAM: £11.5B+ across development productivity (£500M), early failure detection (£10B), and governance automation (£1B). Initial target: 500 UK digital agencies + 200 government bodies + 50 GCC mega-projects.

Status & Roadmap

Currently: Core functionality complete with pilot validation in progress. 2026: Market launch targeting UK digital agencies and government bodies. Path to Exit: £100M+ valuation by 2028-2030 (acquisition or scale as independent SaaS).

Adaptive Early Childhood Learning Platform

Core Features

✓ Adaptive Learning

Personalizes content based on child's progress and learning style

✓ Interactive Experiences

Engaging games, stories, and activities for developmental milestones

✓ Parent Dashboard

Real-time insights into child's learning progress

✓ Multi-Curriculum

Supports multiple educational frameworks and languages

✓ Teacher Integration

Seamless coordination between home and school learning

✓ Child Safety

Built with privacy and safety as core principles

Market Position

B2C platform targeting parents and B2B partnerships with schools and educational institutions. Global EdTech market opportunity with focus on emerging markets and developed world premium segment.
